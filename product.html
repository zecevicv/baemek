About the product & Nutrition

WPH (Whey Protein Hydrolysate)

K-PRO WPH is manufactured by a high "state of the art" process for treated whey, using "Chalav Israel" fresh whey with high kosher supervision under a strict and modern quality system.

WPH is a suitable ingredient for use in various nutritional and functional applications for example infant formulas, sport nutrition, acid beverages, protein bars and nutritional fortification.

Baâ€™emek's K-PRO WPH is a superior protein source which has a low flavor profile, excellent amino acid composition and high digestibility. It is heat stable, vegetarian, Kosher and Halal approval.

WPC (Whey Protein Concentrate)

K-PRO WPC is manufactured by a state-of-the-art process for treated whey, using "Chalav Israel" fresh whey with high kosher supervision under a strict and modern quality system. WPC is a high functional ingredient in which the protein level varies from 34% to 80% - both sweet and lactic WPC. WPC is an essential ingredient for use in a wide variety of applications such as ice cream, yogurt, dairy desserts, cakes and bakery products.

WPC is also considered as a superior source of protein fortification for nutritional products, infant formulas, and special formulas for elderly people. Under our extremely hygienic conditions, the company supplies infant food producers in Israel.
