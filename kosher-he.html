WPC (Whey Protein Hydrolysate)

K-PRO WPC is manufactured by a state-of-the-art process for treated whey, using "Chalav Israel" fresh whey with high kosher supervision under a strict and modern quality system. WPC is a high functional ingredient in which the protein level varies from 34% to 80% - both sweet and lactic WPC. WPC is an essential ingredient for use in a wide variety of applications such as ice cream, yogurt, dairy desserts, cakes and bakery products. WPC is also considered as a superior source of protein fortification for nutritional products, infant formulas, and special formulas for elderly people. Under our extremely hygienic conditions, the company supplies infant food producers in Israel. For any further information about our K-PRO WPC products, please contact us
